Results

We used our model that predict the outcome of a game to simulate the whole World Cup competition. A simulation starts with the initial groups defined by the FIFA draw and simulates each game of the group phase in order to determine which teams will access the knockout phase. Then, we follow the rules of the FIFA World Cup to determine which team plays against which team in the knockout phase, and we simulate the games until the final.
